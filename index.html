Scrambled egg recipe:-

Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.



Ingredients:-
  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream
Method:-
  1. Crack 4 large eggs into a bowl, add salt and pepper to taste, and whisk until fully combined.
  2. Melt 2 tablespoons of butter or oil in a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat.
  3. our the whisked eggs into the pan and let them sit until they start to set around the edges.
  4. Using a spatula, gently push the eggs from the edges towards the center, stirring occasionally until mostly set but still slightly runny.
  5. Remove from heat just before fully cooked, giving a final stir, then serve immediately.
